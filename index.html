About the Campaign

Here, I connect with as many people as possible to spread awareness about climate change.

In this campaign, my goal is to meet and engage with individuals publicly, sharing insights about climate change and capturing selfies with them. Each selfie I take will be uploaded to my Instagram page, which you can follow to see.

Why Selfies?
I believe that every selfie I take is a powerful symbol of spreading awareness. The more selfies I capture, the more I amplify the message about the urgent need for climate action. It's not just about the pictures; it's about sparking conversations and inspiring others to take action.

My Purpose?
The reason behind this initiative is simple: to create a better future. Globally, our lifestyle has reached a point where we often forget to value our environment. It's alarming that many fail to realize the consequences of our current lifestyle choices on our future existence.

Nature has so much to teach us, and it's crucial that we strive to enhance our relationship with it. By promoting environmental consciousness through GreenSelfieCampaign, I aim to encourage sustainable practices and foster a deeper appreciation for our natural world.

Join me on this journey towards a greener future and to spread awareness and encourage sustainable living. Together, let's make every selfie count for the planet.
